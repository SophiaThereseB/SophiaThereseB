What is Art?

Art is the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination. It can be in numerous forms including:

  • Painting
  • Literature
  • Dance
  • Theater
  • Music

Primarily, art is appreciated for its beauty and/or emotional power. This website focuses on fine arts like paintings, sculptures, and more.


History of Art

Truthfully, no one really knows how, when, or where the first piece of art was kindled. However, somewhere, somehow, it was. As far as we know, it all began with medieval art. Medieval art refers to a period known as the Middle Ages, which spanned from 476 A.D. to the 14th century. Art produced during the Middle Ages was based on the Roman Empire, the early Christian church, and the "barbarian" culture of Northern Europe. The medieval art period sprouted into 13 other art periods including the Renaissance, impressionism, cubism, and contemporary art.

Artists

Artists are people who create and produce art. There are millions of unique artists all around the world, some are well-known and others are not. This website focuses on two well-known artists -- Vincent van Gogh and Michelangelo -- from different art periods, along with their art styles.
